The Time of Ancestors: ECQ Rundown Top 8 from the event

Follow @shiftstoned

November 18, 2019 - The first Eternal Chapionship Qualifier event of the 2020 season has concluded, with Xenan Cultists emerging victorious despite a target painted on their backs. Nahid's Faithful and friends composed an overwhelming sixty percent of the field, with a proportionate share of the Top 8.

The remaining non-Cultist lists in the event appeared custom-built to counter this boogeyman of the format. However — while a single archetype was predominant — no two builds among the finishers were the same. Here we take a look at the construction of the winning decks, highlighting the standout features of each.

1st  P L A C E:

► BruisedByGod's Deck (Xenan Cultists)

16T, 21S, E-Makkar x4, Prohpet x4, V-favor x4, Ark of Sol [M]
Seek Power Gaming's BruisedByGod returns to the competitive spotlight of Eternal in style following a hiatus. Like all of the lists seen here, BBG's build features a full playset of High Prophet of Sol along with an Ark of Sol in the Market. But they differed in how they constructed the rest of their Power sources. BBG opts for a full set bot of Vara's Favor and Emblem of Makkar with an Ark of Sol in the Market.

Standout feature: Four Vara's Favors. Swuirl the Sands in Market proved relevant in at least on matchup during the semi-finals.

2nd  P L A C E:

► AntMan's Deck (Hooru Tempo)

15J, 18P: 2x Emerald Coin, Emblem-L [M]
Perhaps the most innovative build of the finishers, seemed specifically constructed to counter Cultists. Particularly spicy. Appears to be built specifically with the intention of putting Cultists in the crosshairs.

Standout feature: Only deck to feature Coins, makese sense with Weapon synergies of Units such as Axe Sharpener, Imperial Loyalist and speed up Mastery for Varret and Blitrock. Curse based removal dodges Nahid's Choice. Overwhelm from Savagery counters chump blockers.
